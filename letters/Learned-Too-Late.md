# Things I Learned Too Late

- Saying “No” is not selfish. Staying silent when you’re being used is.
- People will treat you exactly how much you allow them to.
- Your value is not in how useful you are to others.
- Loneliness is better than fake friendships.
- Discipline > Motivation.
- The quality of your life is determined by the quality of your boundaries.
- Most people don’t actually care as much as you think they do.
- Your parents are getting older faster than you realize.

I learned these things the hard way.

I hope you don’t have to.

— Bobby
