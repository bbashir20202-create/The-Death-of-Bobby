# How I Want To Be Remembered

When I die, I don’t want to be remembered as successful.

I want to be remembered as someone who:

- Finally learned to say “No”
- Turned his pain into something useful
- Was brutally honest with himself in public
- Built something real from nothing
- Helped even one young person avoid the same mistakes he made

I want people to say:
“He was messy. He was late. But he never stopped trying to become better.”

That’s enough.

— Bobby
