# My Final Apology

To everyone I hurt, disappointed, or let down:

I’m sorry.

I’m sorry for every time I said “yes” when I wanted to say “no”.  
I’m sorry for being unreliable.  
I’m sorry for disappearing when things got hard.  
I’m sorry for letting fear control me for so many years.

I spent a long time being a people-pleaser because I was scared of being abandoned.  
That fear made me do stupid things and hurt people who didn’t deserve it.

If I hurt you, I want you to know it was never because I didn’t care.  
It was because I didn’t know how to care for myself.

I hope you can forgive me.  
And more importantly, I hope you can forgive the version of me that didn’t know any better.

I tried to become better before I left.

— Bobby
