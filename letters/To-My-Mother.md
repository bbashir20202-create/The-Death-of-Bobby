# Letter to My Mother

Mom,

If you're reading this, then I’ve already left.

First, I want to say thank you.  
You carried me when I was weak. You fed me when we had nothing. You prayed for me even when I was lost, angry, and disrespectful.

I’m sorry for every time I shouted at you.  
I’m sorry for every time I made you cry because I was too selfish to see your pain.  
I’m sorry I became cold during my worst years.

I hope you know that even in my darkest times, I never stopped loving you. I was just too broken to show it properly.

My greatest wish is that you live a long, peaceful life surrounded by people who treat you with the respect you deserve.

Please don’t mourn me too long.  
Eat your favorite food. Watch your dramas. Laugh again.

I am okay now.

Your son,  
Bobby
