# Living Will & Digital Legacy

**Last Updated:** 27 May 2026

### This is my Living Will.

If I die, this document should be read and followed.

---

### 1. My Digital Legacy

- This entire GitHub repository (`The-Death-of-Bobby`) should remain public forever.
- My family or closest friend has permission to update the "Last Updated" date and add a final note if they wish.
- All letters in this repository are my final words. They should not be edited or removed.

### 2. My Wishes

- I do **not** want a big funeral.
- I want a small gathering with the people who actually cared about me.
- Read the file `legacy/Last-Message-To-The-World.md` out loud.

- Do **not** let people say I was "a good guy".  
  Tell them the truth — I was messy, late, and full of fear for many years… but I kept trying to become better.

### 3. My Belongings

- All my money and assets should go to my mother first.
- If she is no longer here, split it between my siblings and charity.
- My laptop and phone should be wiped and given to someone who needs them.
- My journals and notebooks should be burned (except this GitHub repository).

### 4. Final Request

Do not mourn me for too long.

Instead, live better than I did.

Say “No” when you need to.  
Protect your peace.  
Document your journey.  
Be honest with yourself.

That is the best way to honor me.

---

**Signed with love and clarity,**  
**Bobby**
